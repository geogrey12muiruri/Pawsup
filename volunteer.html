Volunteering Requirements

  • Must be at least 16 years of age. If you are under 16, you must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.
  • Must commit to at least 2 hours of volunteering per week
  • Reliability and commitment to fulfilling scheduled shifts
  • Completion of a volunteer application form

How to Apply

Ready to make a difference? Applying to volunteer at Paw's Up Animal Shelter is easy! Simply fill out our online volunteer application form, and our team will get in touch with you to discuss further steps. We also encourage you to reach out if you have any questions or need more information. Together, we can create a better world for animals!
